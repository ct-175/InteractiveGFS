Early History

The UK has been inhabited for thousands of years. Important eras include:

  • Stone Age
  • Bronze Age
  • Iron Age

Various tribes and cultures shaped early Britain.

The Roman Era

Romans invaded Britain in 43 AD and built roads, baths, and Hadrian's Wall.

Fun Fact: London was founded by the Romans and called Londinium!

The Middle Ages

Key events:

  • The Norman Conquest (1066)
  • Magna Carta signed (1215)
  • Hundred Years' War (1337–1453)

Answer: 1215

The Tudor Period

Famous monarchs:

  • Henry VIII – created the Church of England
  • Elizabeth I – defeated the Spanish Armada

The Industrial Revolution

From the 18th century, Britain became the world leader in industry:

  • Steam engines and factories
  • Urbanization
  • Major social changes

The 20th Century

Major events:

  • World War I and II
  • Formation of the NHS (1948)
  • Decolonization of the British Empire

Modern UK

Today, the UK is a constitutional monarchy and a parliamentary democracy.

Important facts:

  • Prime Minister is the head of government
  • Queen/King is the head of state
  • Member of the Commonwealth
